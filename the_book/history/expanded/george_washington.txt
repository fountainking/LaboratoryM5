George Washington

George Washington was a Founding Father
and the first president of the United
States, serving from 1789 to 1797. As
commander of the Continental Army,
Washington led Patriot forces to
victory in the American Revolutionary
War against the British Empire. He is
commonly known as the Father of the
Nation for his role in bringing about
American independence.
