Ottoman Empire

The Ottoman Empire, also called the
Turkish Empire, was an empire that
controlled much of Southeast Europe,
West Asia, and North Africa from the
14th to early 20th centuries; it also
controlled parts of southeastern
Central Europe between the early 16th
and early 18th centuries.
