Brazil

Brazil, officially the Federative
Republic of Brazil, is the largest
country in South America. It is also
the world's fifth-largest country by
area and the seventh-largest by
population, with over 213 million
people. The country is a federation
composed of 26 states and a Federal
District, which hosts the capital,
Braslia. Its most populous city is So
Paulo, followed by Rio de Janeiro.
Brazil has the most Portuguese speakers
in the world and is the only country in
the Americas where Portuguese is an
official language.
