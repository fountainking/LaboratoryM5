Light

Light, visible light, or visible
radiation is electromagnetic radiation
that can be perceived by the human eye.
Visible light spans the visible
spectrum and is usually defined as
having wavelengths in the range of
400700 nanometres (nm), corresponding
to frequencies of 750420 terahertz. The
visible band sits adjacent to the
infrared and the ultraviolet, called
collectively optical radiation.
