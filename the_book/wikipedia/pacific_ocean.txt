Pacific Ocean

The Pacific Ocean is the largest and
deepest of Earth's five oceanic
divisions. It extends from the Arctic
Ocean in the north to the Southern
Ocean, or, depending on the definition,
to Antarctica in the south, and is
bounded by the continents of Asia and
Australia in the west and the Americas
in the east.
