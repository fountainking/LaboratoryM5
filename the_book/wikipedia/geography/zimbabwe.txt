Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe, officially the Republic of
Zimbabwe, is a landlocked country in
Southeast Africa, between the Zambezi
and Limpopo Rivers, bordered by South
Africa to the south, Botswana to the
southwest, Zambia to the north, and
Mozambique to the east. The capital and
largest city is Harare, and the second
largest is Bulawayo.
