Athens

Athens is the capital and largest city
of Greece. A significant coastal urban
area in the Mediterranean, Athens is
also the capital of the Attica region
and is the southernmost capital on the
European mainland. With its urban
area's population numbering over 3.6
million, it is the eighth-largest urban
area in the European Union (EU). The
Municipality of Athens, which
constitutes a small administrative unit
of the entire urban area, had a
population of 643,452 in 2021, within
its official limits, and a land area of
38.96 km2 (15.04 sq mi).
