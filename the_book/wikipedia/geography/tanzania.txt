Tanzania

Tanzania, officially the United
Republic of Tanzania, is a country in
East Africa within the African Great
Lakes region. It is bordered by Uganda
to the northwest; Kenya to the
northeast; the Indian Ocean to the
east; Mozambique and Malawi to the
south; Zambia to the southwest; and
Rwanda, Burundi, and the Democratic
Republic of the Congo to the west.
According to a 2024 estimate, Tanzania
has a population of around 67.5
million, making it the most populous
country located entirely south of the
equator.
