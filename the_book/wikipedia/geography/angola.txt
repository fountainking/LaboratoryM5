Angola

Angola, officially the Republic of
Angola, is a country on the
west-central coast of Southern Africa.
It is the second-largest
Portuguese-speaking (Lusophone) country
after Brazil in both total area and
population and is the seventh-largest
country in Africa. It is bordered by
Namibia to the south, the Democratic
Republic of the Congo to the north,
Zambia to the east, and the Atlantic
Ocean to the west. Angola has an
exclave province, the province of
Cabinda, that borders the Republic of
the Congo and the Democratic Republic
of the Congo. The capital and most
populous city is Luanda.
