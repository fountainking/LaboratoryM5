Algeria

Algeria, officially the People's
Democratic Republic of Algeria, is a
country in the Maghreb region of North
Africa. It is bordered to the northeast
by Tunisia; to the east by Libya; to
the southeast by Niger; to the
southwest by Mali, Mauritania, and
Western Sahara; to the west by Morocco;
and to the north by the Mediterranean
Sea. The capital and largest city is
Algiers, located in the far north on
the Mediterranean coast.
