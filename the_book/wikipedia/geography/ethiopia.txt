Ethiopia

Ethiopia, officially the Federal
Democratic Republic of Ethiopia, is a
landlocked country located in the Horn
of Africa region of East Africa. It
shares borders with Eritrea to the
north, Djibouti to the northeast,
Somalia to the east, Kenya to the
south, South Sudan to the west, and
Sudan to the northwest. Ethiopia covers
a land area of 1,104,300 square
kilometres (426,400 sq mi). As of 2025,
it has around 135 million inhabitants,
making it the tenth-most populous
country. The national capital and
largest city, Addis Ababa, lies several
kilometres west of the East African
Rift that splits the country into the
African and Somali tectonic plates.
