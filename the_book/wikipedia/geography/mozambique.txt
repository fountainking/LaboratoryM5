Mozambique

Mozambique, officially the Republic of
Mozambique, is a country located in
Southeast Africa bordered by the Indian
Ocean to the east, Tanzania to the
north, Malawi and Zambia to the
northwest, Zimbabwe to the west, and
Eswatini and South Africa to the south
and southwest. The sovereign state is
separated from the Comoros, Mayotte,
and Madagascar through the Mozambique
Channel to the east. The capital and
largest city is Maputo.
