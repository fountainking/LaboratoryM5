Grenada

Grenada is an island country of the
West Indies in the eastern Caribbean
Sea. The southernmost of the Windward
Islands, Grenada is directly south of
Saint Vincent and the Grenadines and
about 100 miles (160 km) north of
Trinidad and the South American
mainland.
