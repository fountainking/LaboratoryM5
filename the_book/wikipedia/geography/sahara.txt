Sahara

The Sahara is a desert spanning across
North Africa. With an area of 9,200,000
square kilometres (3,600,000 sq mi), it
is the largest hot desert in the world
and the third-largest desert overall,
smaller only than the deserts of
Antarctica and the northern Arctic.
