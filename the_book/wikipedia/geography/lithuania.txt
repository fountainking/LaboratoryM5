Lithuania

Lithuania, officially the Republic of
Lithuania, is a country in the Baltic
region of Europe. It is one of three
Baltic states and lies on the eastern
shore of the Baltic Sea, bordered by
Latvia to the north, Belarus to the
east and south, Poland to the south,
and the Russian semi-exclave of
Kaliningrad Oblast to the southwest,
with a maritime border with Sweden to
the west. Lithuania covers an area of
65,300 km2 (25,200 sq mi), with a
population of 2.9 million. Its capital
and largest city is Vilnius; other
major cities include Kaunas, Klaipėda,
Šiauliai and Panevėžys. Lithuanians are
the titular nation, belong to the
ethnolinguistic group of Balts, and
speak Lithuanian.
