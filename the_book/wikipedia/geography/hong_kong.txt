Hong Kong

Hong Kong is a special administrative
region of China. Situated on China's
southern coast just south of Shenzhen,
it consists of Hong Kong Island,
Kowloon, and the New Territories. With
7.5 million residents in a
1,114-square-kilometre (430 sq mi)
territory, Hong Kong is the fourth-most
densely populated region in the world.
