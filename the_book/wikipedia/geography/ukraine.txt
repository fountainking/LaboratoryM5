Ukraine

Ukraine is a country in Eastern Europe.
It is the second-largest country in
Europe after Russia, which borders it
to the east and northeast. Ukraine also
borders Belarus to the north; Poland
and Slovakia to the west; Hungary,
Romania and Moldova to the southwest;
and the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov
to the south and southeast. Kyiv is the
nation's capital and largest city,
followed by Kharkiv, Odesa, and Dnipro.
Ukraine's official language is
Ukrainian.
