Vienna

Vienna is the capital, most populous
city, and one of nine states of
Austria. It is Austria's primate city,
with just over two million inhabitants.
Its larger metropolitan area has a
population of nearly 2.9 million,
representing nearly one-third of the
country's population. Vienna is the
cultural, economic, and political
center of the country, the
fifth-largest city by population in the
European Union, and the most populous
of the cities on the river Danube.
