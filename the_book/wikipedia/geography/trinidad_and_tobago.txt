Trinidad and Tobago

Trinidad and Tobago, officially the
Republic of Trinidad and Tobago, is the
southernmost island country in the
Caribbean, comprising the main islands
of Trinidad and Tobago, along with
several smaller islets. The capital
city is Port of Spain, while its
largest and most populous municipality
is Chaguanas. Despite its proximity to
South America, being on its continental
shelf, Trinidad and Tobago is generally
considered to be part of the Caribbean.
