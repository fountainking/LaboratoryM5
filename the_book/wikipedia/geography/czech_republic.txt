Czech Republic

The Czech Republic, also known as
Czechia and historically known as
Bohemia, is a landlocked country in
Central Europe. The country is bordered
by Austria to the south, Germany to the
west, Poland to the northeast, and
Slovakia to the southeast. The Czech
Republic has a hilly landscape that
covers an area of 78,871 square
kilometers (30,452 sq mi) with a mostly
temperate continental and oceanic
climate. The capital and largest city
is Prague; other major cities and urban
areas include Brno, Ostrava, Plzeň and
Liberec.
