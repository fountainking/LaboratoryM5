Palau

Palau, officially the Republic of
Palau, is an island country in the
Micronesia subregion of Oceania in the
western Pacific Ocean. The Republic of
Palau consists of approximately 340
islands and is the western part of the
Caroline Islands, while the eastern and
central parts make up the Federated
States of Micronesia.
