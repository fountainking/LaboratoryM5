Mont Blanc

Mont Blanc is a mountain in the Alps,
rising 4,805.59 m (15,766 ft) above sea
level, located right at the
Franco-Italian border. It is the
highest mountain in Europe outside the
Caucasus Mountains, the second-most
prominent mountain in Europe, and the
11th most prominent mountain in the
world.
