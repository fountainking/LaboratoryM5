Belgium

Belgium, officially the Kingdom of
Belgium, is a country in Northwestern
Europe. Situated in a coastal lowland
region known as the Low Countries, it
is bordered by the Netherlands to the
north, Germany to the east, Luxembourg
to the southeast, France to the south,
and the North Sea to the west. Belgium
covers an area of 30,689 km2 (11,849 sq
mi) and has a population of more than
11.8 million; its population density of
383/km2 (990/sq mi) ranks 22nd in the
world and sixth in Europe. The capital
and largest metropolitan region is
Brussels; other major cities are
Antwerp, Ghent, Charleroi, Liège,
Bruges, Namur, and Leuven.
