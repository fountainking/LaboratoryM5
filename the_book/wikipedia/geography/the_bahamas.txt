The Bahamas

The Bahamas, officially the
Commonwealth of The Bahamas, is an
island country located within the
Lucayan Archipelago in the Atlantic
Ocean. It contains 97 per cent of the
archipelago's land area and 88 per cent
of its population. It comprises more
than 3,000 islands, cays and islets in
the Atlantic Ocean, located north of
Cuba and north-west of the island of
Hispaniola and the Turks and Caicos
Islands, southeast of the U.S. state of
Florida and east of the Florida Keys.
The capital and largest city is Nassau
on the island of New Providence. The
Royal Bahamas Defence Force describes
the Bahamas' territory as encompassing
470,000 km2 (180,000 sq mi) of ocean
space.
