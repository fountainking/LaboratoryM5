Turkey

Turkey, officially the Republic of
Türkiye, is a country mainly located in
Anatolia in West Asia, with a
relatively small part called East
Thrace in Southeast Europe. It borders
the Black Sea to the north; Georgia,
Armenia, Azerbaijan, and Iran to the
east; Iraq, Syria, and the
Mediterranean Sea to the south; and the
Aegean Sea, Greece, and Bulgaria to the
west. Turkey is home to over 85 million
people; most are ethnic Turks, while
Kurds are the largest ethnic minority.
Officially a secular state, Turkey has
a Muslim-majority population. Ankara is
Turkey's capital and second-largest
city. Istanbul is its largest city and
economic center. Other major cities
include İzmir, Bursa, and Antalya.
