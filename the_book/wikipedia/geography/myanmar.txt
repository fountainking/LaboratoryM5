Myanmar

Myanmar, officially the Republic of the
Union of Myanmar and also referred to
as Burma, is a country in northwest
Southeast Asia. It is the largest
country by area in Mainland Southeast
Asia and has a population of about 55
million. It is bordered by India and
Bangladesh to the northwest, China to
the northeast, Laos and Thailand to the
east and southeast, and the Andaman Sea
and the Bay of Bengal to the south and
southwest. The country's capital city
is Naypyidaw, while its largest city is
Yangon.
