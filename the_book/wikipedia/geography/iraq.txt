Iraq

Iraq, officially the Republic of Iraq,
is a country in West Asia. It is
bordered by Saudi Arabia to the south,
Turkey to the north, Iran to the east,
the Persian Gulf and Kuwait to the
southeast, Jordan to the southwest, and
Syria to the west. The country covers
an area of 438,317 square kilometres
(169,235 sq mi) and has a population of
over 46 million, making it the 58th
largest country by area and the 31st
most populous in the world. Baghdad,
home to over 8 million people, is the
capital city and the largest in the
country.
