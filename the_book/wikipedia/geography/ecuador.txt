Ecuador

Ecuador, officially the Republic of
Ecuador, is a country in northwestern
South America, bordered by Colombia on
the north, Peru on the east and south,
and the Pacific Ocean on the west. It
also includes the Galápagos Province
which contains the Galápagos Islands in
the Pacific, about 1,000 kilometers
(621 mi) west of the mainland. The
country's capital is Quito and its
largest city is Guayaquil.
