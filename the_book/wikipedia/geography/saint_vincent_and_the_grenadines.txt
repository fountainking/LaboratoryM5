Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines,
sometimes known simply as Saint Vincent
or SVG, is an island country in the
eastern Caribbean. It is located in the
southeast Windward Islands of the
Lesser Antilles, which lie in the West
Indies, at the southern end of the
eastern border between the Caribbean
Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. To the
north lies Saint Lucia, to the east is
Barbados, and Grenada lies to the
south.
