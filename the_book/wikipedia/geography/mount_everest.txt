Mount Everest

Mount Everest is Earth's highest
mountain above sea level. It lies in
the Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the
Himalayas and marks part of the
China–Nepal border at its summit. Its
height was most recently measured in
2020 by Chinese and Nepali authorities
as 8,848.86 m.
