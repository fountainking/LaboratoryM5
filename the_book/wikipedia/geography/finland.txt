Finland

Finland, officially the Republic of
Finland, is a Nordic country in
Northern Europe. It borders Sweden to
the northwest, Norway to the north, and
Russia to the east, with the Gulf of
Bothnia to the west and the Gulf of
Finland to the south, opposite Estonia.
Finland has a population of 5.6
million, the majority being ethnic
Finns. Its capital and largest city is
Helsinki. The official languages are
Finnish and Swedish, the mother tongues
of 84.1 percent and 5.1 percent of the
population, respectively. Finland's
climate varies from humid continental
in the south to boreal in the north.
Its land is predominantly covered by
boreal forest, with over 180,000
recorded lakes.
