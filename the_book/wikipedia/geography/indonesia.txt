Indonesia

Indonesia, officially the Republic of
Indonesia, is a country in Southeast
Asia and Oceania, between the Indian
and Pacific oceans. Comprising over
17,000 islands, including Sumatra,
Java, Sulawesi, and parts of Borneo and
New Guinea, Indonesia is the world's
largest archipelagic state and the
14th-largest country by area, at
1,904,569 square kilometres. With over
280 million people, Indonesia is the
world's fourth-most-populous country
and the most populous Muslim-majority
country. Java, the world's most
populous island, is home to more than
half of the country's population.
