The Gambia

The Gambia, officially the Republic of
The Gambia, is a country in West
Africa. Geographically, The Gambia is
the smallest country in continental
Africa; it is surrounded by Senegal on
all sides except for the western part,
which is bordered by the Atlantic
Ocean.
