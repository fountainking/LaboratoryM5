Republic of the Congo

The Republic of the Congo, also known
as Congo-Brazzaville, the Congo
Republic or simply the Congo, is a
country located on the western coast of
Central Africa to the west of the Congo
River. It is bordered to the west by
Gabon, to the northwest by Cameroon, to
the northeast by the Central African
Republic, to the southeast by the
Democratic Republic of the Congo, to
the south by the Angolan exclave of
Cabinda, and to the southwest by the
Atlantic Ocean.
