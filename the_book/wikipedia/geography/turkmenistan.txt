Turkmenistan

Turkmenistan is a landlocked country in
Central Asia, bordered by Kazakhstan to
the north, Uzbekistan to the north,
east and northeast, Afghanistan to the
southeast, Iran to the south and
southwest, and the Caspian Sea to the
west. It is one of six independent
Turkic states. Ashgabat is the capital
and largest city. With over 7 million
people, Turkmenistan is the 35th
most-populous country in Asia and has
the lowest population of the Central
Asian republics while being one of the
most sparsely populated nations located
on the Asian continent.
