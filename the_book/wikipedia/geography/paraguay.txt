Paraguay

Paraguay, officially the Republic of
Paraguay, is a landlocked country
located in the central region of South
America. It borders Bolivia to the
northwest and north, Brazil to the
northeast and east, and Argentina to
the southeast, south, and west.
Paraguay has access to the Atlantic
Ocean via the Paraná–Paraguay Waterway.
The country is governed as a unitary
presidential republic composed of a
capital district and seventeen
departments. Its capital and largest
city is Asunción.
