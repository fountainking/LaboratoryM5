Panama

Panama, officially the Republic of
Panama, is a country located at the
southern end of Central America,
bordering South America. It is bordered
by Costa Rica to the west, Colombia to
the southeast, the Caribbean Sea to the
north, and the Pacific Ocean to the
south. Its capital and largest city is
Panama City, whose metropolitan area is
home to nearly half of the country's
over 4 million inhabitants.
