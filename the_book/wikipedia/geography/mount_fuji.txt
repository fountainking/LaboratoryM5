Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is an active stratovolcano
located on the Japanese island of
Honshu, with a summit elevation of
3,776.24 m. It is the highest mountain
in Japan, the second-highest volcano on
any Asian island, and seventh-highest
peak of an island on Earth.
