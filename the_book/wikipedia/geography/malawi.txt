Malawi

Malawi, officially the Republic of
Malawi, is a landlocked country in
Southeastern Africa. It is bordered by
Zambia to the west, Tanzania to the
north and northeast, and Mozambique to
the east, south, and southwest. Malawi
spans over 118,484 km2 (45,747 sq mi)
and has an estimated population of
22,224,282. Lilongwe is its capital and
largest city, while the next three
largest cities are Blantyre, Mzuzu, and
Zomba, the former capital.
