Germany

Germany, officially the Federal
Republic of Germany, is a country in
Western and Central Europe. It lies
between the Baltic Sea and the North
Sea to the north and the Alps to the
south. Its sixteen constituent states
have a total population of over 82
million, making it the most populous
member state of the European Union.
Germany borders Denmark to the north;
Poland and the Czech Republic to the
east; Austria and Switzerland to the
south; and France, Luxembourg, Belgium,
and the Netherlands to the west. The
nation's capital and most populous city
is Berlin and its main financial centre
is Frankfurt; the largest urban area is
the Ruhr.
