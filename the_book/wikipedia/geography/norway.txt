Norway

Norway, officially the Kingdom of
Norway, is a Nordic country located on
the Scandinavian Peninsula in Northern
Europe. The remote Arctic island of Jan
Mayen and the archipelago of Svalbard
also form part of the Kingdom of
Norway. Bouvet Island, located in the
Subantarctic, is a dependency, and not
a part of the Kingdom; Norway also
claims the Antarctic territories of
Peter I Island and Queen Maud Land.
Norway has a population of 5.6 million.
Its capital and largest city is Oslo.
The country has a total area of 385,207
square kilometres (148,729 sq mi). The
country shares a long eastern border
with Sweden, and is bordered by Finland
and Russia to the northeast. Norway has
an extensive coastline facing the
Skagerrak strait, the North Atlantic
Ocean, and the Barents Sea. In 2024,
Norway was ranked the seventh happiest
country in the world.
