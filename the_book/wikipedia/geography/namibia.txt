Namibia

Namibia, officially the Republic of
Namibia, is a country in Southern
Africa. Its borders include the
Atlantic Ocean to the west, Angola and
Zambia to the north, Botswana to the
east and South Africa to the south; in
the northeast, approximating a
quadripoint, Zimbabwe lies less than
200 metres away along the Zambezi River
near Kazungula, Zambia. Namibia's
capital and largest city is Windhoek.
