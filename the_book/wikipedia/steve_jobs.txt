Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs was an American
businessman, inventor, and investor
best known for co-founding the
technology company Apple Inc. Jobs was
also the founder of NeXT and chairman
and majority shareholder of Pixar. He
was a pioneer of the personal computer
revolution of the 1970s and 1980s,
along with his early business partner
and fellow Apple co-founder Steve
Wozniak.
