Natural language processing

Natural language processing (NLP) is
the processing of natural language
information by a computer. The study of
NLP, a subfield of computer science, is
generally associated with artificial
intelligence. NLP is related to
information retrieval, knowledge
representation, computational
linguistics, and more broadly with
linguistics.
