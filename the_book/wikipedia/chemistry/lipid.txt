Lipid

Lipids are a broad group of organic
compounds which include fats, waxes,
sterols, fat-soluble vitamins,
monoglycerides, diglycerides,
phospholipids, and others. The
functions of lipids include storing
energy, signaling, and acting as
structural components of cell
membranes. Lipids have applications in
the cosmetic and food industries, and
in nanotechnology.
