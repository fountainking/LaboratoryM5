Physical chemistry

Physical chemistry is the study of
macroscopic and microscopic phenomena
in chemical systems in terms of the
principles, practices, and concepts of
physics such as motion, energy, force,
time, thermodynamics, quantum
chemistry, statistical mechanics,
analytical dynamics and chemical
equilibria.
