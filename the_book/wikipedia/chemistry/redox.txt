Redox

Redox is a type of chemical reaction in
which the oxidation states of the
reactants change. Oxidation is the loss
of electrons or an increase in the
oxidation state, while reduction is the
gain of electrons or a decrease in the
oxidation state. The oxidation and
reduction processes occur
simultaneously in the chemical
reaction.
