Yoga

Yoga is a group of physical, mental,
and spiritual practices or disciplines
that originated with its own philosophy
in ancient India, aimed at controlling
body and mind to attain various
soteriological goals (moksha), as
practiced in the Hindu, Jain, Sikh, and
Buddhist traditions.
