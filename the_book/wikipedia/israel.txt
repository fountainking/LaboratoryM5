Israel

Israel, officially the State of Israel,
is a country in the Southern Levant
region of West Asia. It borders Lebanon
to the north, Syria to the northeast,
Jordan to the east and Egypt to the
southwest. Israel occupies the
Palestinian territories of the West
Bank in the east and the Gaza Strip in
the southwest, as well as the Syrian
Golan Heights in the northeast.
Israel's western coast lies on the
Mediterranean Sea, the southernmost
point reaches the Red Sea, and the east
includes part of the Dead Sea.
Jerusalem is the government seat and
proclaimed capital, while Tel Aviv is
the country's largest urban area and
economic centre.
