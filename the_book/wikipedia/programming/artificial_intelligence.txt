Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the
capability of computational systems to
perform tasks typically associated with
human intelligence, such as learning,
reasoning, problem-solving, perception,
and decision-making. It is a field of
research in computer science that
develops and studies methods and
software that enable machines to
perceive their environment and use
learning and intelligence to take
actions that maximize their chances of
achieving defined goals.
