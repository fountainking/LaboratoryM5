Kubernetes

Kubernetes, also known as K8s is an
open-source container orchestration
system for automating software
deployment, scaling, and management.
Originally designed by Google, the
project is now maintained by a
worldwide community of contributors,
and the trademark is held by the Cloud
Native Computing Foundation.
