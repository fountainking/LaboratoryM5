Tortoise

Tortoises are reptiles of the family
Testudinidae of the order Testudines.
Like other testudines, tortoises have a
shell to protect from predation and
other threats. The shell in tortoises
is generally hard, and like other
members of the suborder Cryptodira,
they retract their necks and heads
directly backward into the shell to
protect them.
