Mouse

A mouse is a small rodent.
Characteristically, mice are known to
have a pointed snout, small rounded
ears, a body-length scaly tail, and a
high breeding rate. The best known
mouse species is the common house
mouse. Mice are also popular as pets.
In some places, certain kinds of field
mice are locally common. They are known
to invade homes for food and shelter.
