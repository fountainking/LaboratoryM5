Opossum

Opossums are members of the marsupial
order Didelphimorphia endemic to the
Americas. The largest order of
marsupials in the Western Hemisphere,
it comprises 126 species in 18 genera.
Opossums originated in South America
and entered North America in the Great
American Interchange following the
connection of North and South America
in the late Cenozoic.
