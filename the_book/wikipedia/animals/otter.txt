Otter

Otters are carnivorous mammals in the
subfamily Lutrinae. The 14 extant otter
species are all semiaquatic, both
freshwater and marine. Lutrinae is a
branch of the Mustelidae family, which
includes weasels, badgers, mink, and
wolverines, among other animals.
