Walrus

The walrus is a large pinniped marine
mammal with discontinuous distribution
about the North Pole in the Arctic
Ocean and subarctic seas of the
Northern Hemisphere. It is the only
extant species in the family Odobenidae
and genus Odobenus. This species is
subdivided into two subspecies: the
Atlantic walrus, which lives in the
Atlantic Ocean, and the Pacific walrus,
which lives in the Pacific Ocean.
