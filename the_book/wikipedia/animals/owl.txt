Owl

Owls are birds from the order
Strigiformes, which includes over 200
species of mostly solitary and
nocturnal birds of prey typified by an
upright stance, a large, broad head,
binocular vision, binaural hearing,
sharp talons, and feathers adapted for
silent flight. Exceptions include the
diurnal northern hawk-owl and the
gregarious burrowing owl.
