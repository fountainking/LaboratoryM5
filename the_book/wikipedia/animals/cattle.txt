Cattle

Cattle are large, domesticated, bovid
ungulates widely kept as livestock.
They are prominent modern members of
the subfamily Bovinae and the most
widespread species of the genus Bos.
Mature female cattle are called cows
and mature male cattle are bulls. Young
female cattle are called heifers, young
male cattle are bullocks, and castrated
male cattle are known as oxen or
steers.
