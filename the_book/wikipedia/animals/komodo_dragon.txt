Komodo dragon

The Komodo dragon, also known as the
Komodo monitor, is a large reptile of
the monitor lizard family Varanidae
that is endemic to the Indonesian
islands of Komodo, Rinca, Flores, Gili
Dasami, and Gili Motang. The largest
extant population lives within the
Komodo National Park in Eastern
Indonesia. It is the largest extant
species of lizard, with the males
growing to a maximum length of 3 m (10
ft) and weighing up to 150 kg (330 lb).
