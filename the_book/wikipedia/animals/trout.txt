Trout

Trout is a generic common name for
numerous species of carnivorous
freshwater fishes belonging to the
genera Oncorhynchus, Salmo, and
Salvelinus, all of which are members of
the subfamily Salmoninae in the family
Salmonidae. The word trout is also used
for some similar-shaped but
non-salmonid fish, such as the spotted
seatrout/speckled trout.
