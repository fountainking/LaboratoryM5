Reindeer

The reindeer or caribou is a species of
deer with circumpolar distribution,
native to Arctic, subarctic, tundra,
boreal, and mountainous regions of
Northern Europe, Siberia, and North
America. It is the only representative
of the genus Rangifer. More recent
studies suggest the splitting of
reindeer and caribou into six distinct
species over their range.
