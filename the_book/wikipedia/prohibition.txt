Prohibition

Prohibition is the act or practice of
forbidding something by law; more
particularly the term refers to the
banning of the manufacture, storage,
transportation, sale, possession, and
consumption of alcoholic beverages. The
word is also used to refer to a period
of time during which such bans are
enforced.
