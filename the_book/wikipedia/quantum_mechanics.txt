Quantum mechanics

Quantum mechanics is the fundamental
physical theory that describes the
behavior of matter and of light; its
unusual characteristics typically occur
at and below the scale of atoms. It is
the foundation of all quantum physics,
which includes quantum chemistry,
quantum biology, quantum field theory,
quantum technology, and quantum
information science.
