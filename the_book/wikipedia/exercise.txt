Exercise

Exercise or working out is physical
activity that enhances or maintains
fitness and overall health. It is
performed for various reasons,
including weight loss or maintenance,
to aid growth and improve strength,
develop muscles and the cardiovascular
system, hone athletic skills, improve
health, or simply for enjoyment. Many
people choose to exercise outdoors
where they can congregate in groups,
socialize, and improve well-being as
well as mental health.
