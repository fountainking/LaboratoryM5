Ash

Ash is the solid remnants of fires.
Specifically, ash refers to all
non-aqueous, non-gaseous residues that
remain after something burns. In
analytical chemistry, to analyse the
mineral and metal content of chemical
samples, ash is the non-gaseous,
non-liquid residue after complete
combustion.
