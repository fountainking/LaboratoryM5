Garlic

Garlic is a species of bulbous
flowering plants in the genus Allium.
Its close relatives include the onion,
shallot, leek, chives, Welsh onion, and
Chinese onion. Garlic is native to
central and south Asia, stretching from
the Black Sea through the southern
Caucasus, northeastern Iran, and the
Hindu Kush; it also grows wild in parts
of Mediterranean Europe. There are two
subspecies and hundreds of varieties of
garlic.
