Anesthesia

Anesthesia or anaesthesia is a state of
controlled, temporary loss of sensation
or awareness that is induced for
medical or veterinary purposes. It may
include some or all of analgesia,
paralysis, amnesia, and
unconsciousness. An individual under
the effects of anesthetic drugs is
referred to as being anesthetized.
