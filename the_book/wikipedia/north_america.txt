North America

North America is a continent in the
Northern and Western hemispheres. North
America is bordered to the north by the
Arctic Ocean, to the east by the
Atlantic Ocean, to the southeast by
South America and the Caribbean Sea,
and to the south and west by the
Pacific Ocean. The region includes
Middle America and Northern America.
