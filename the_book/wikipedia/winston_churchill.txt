Winston Churchill

Sir Winston Leonard Spencer Churchill
was a British statesman, military
officer, and writer who was Prime
Minister of the United Kingdom from
1940 to 1945 and again from 1951 to
1955. For some 62 of the years between
1900 and 1964, he was a member of
parliament (MP) and represented a total
of five constituencies over that time.
Ideologically an adherent to economic
liberalism and imperialism, he was for
most of his career a member of the
Conservative Party, which he led from
1940 to 1955. He was a member of the
Liberal Party from 1904 to 1924.
