Thomas Aquinas

Thomas Aquinas was an Italian Dominican
friar and priest, the foremost
Scholastic thinker, as well as one of
the most influential philosophers and
theologians in the Western tradition. A
Doctor of the Church, he was from the
county of Aquino in the Kingdom of
Sicily.
