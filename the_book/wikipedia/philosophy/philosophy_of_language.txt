Philosophy of language

Philosophy of language is the
philosophical study of the nature of
language. It investigates the
relationship between language, language
users, and the world. Investigations
may include inquiry into the nature of
meaning, intentionality, reference, the
constitution of sentences, concepts,
learning, and thought.
