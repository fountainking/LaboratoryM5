Mechanical engineering

Mechanical engineering is the study of
physical machines and mechanisms that
may involve force and movement. It is
an engineering branch that combines
engineering physics and mathematics
principles with materials science, to
design, analyze, manufacture, and
maintain mechanical systems. It is one
of the oldest and broadest of the
engineering branches.
