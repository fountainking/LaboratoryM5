Wheel

A wheel is a rotating component that is
intended to turn on an axle bearing.
The wheel is one of the key components
of the wheel and axle which is one of
the six simple machines. Wheels, in
conjunction with axles, allow heavy
objects to be moved easily facilitating
movement or transportation while
supporting a load, or performing labor
in machines. Wheels are also used for
other purposes, such as a ship's wheel,
steering wheel, potter's wheel, and
flywheel.
