Tim Berners-Lee

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee, also
known as TimBL, is an English computer
scientist best known as the inventor of
the World Wide Web, HTML, the URL
system, and HTTP. He is a professorial
research fellow at the University of
Oxford and a professor emeritus at the
Massachusetts Institute of Technology
**(MIT).**
