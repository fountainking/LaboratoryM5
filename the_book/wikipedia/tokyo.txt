Tokyo

Tokyo, officially the Tokyo Metropolis,
is the capital and most populous city
in Japan. With a population of over 14
million in the city proper in 2023, it
is one of the most populous urban areas
in the world. The Greater Tokyo Area,
which includes Tokyo and parts of six
neighboring prefectures, is the most
populous metropolitan area in the
world, with 41 million residents as of
2024.
