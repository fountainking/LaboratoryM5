Victoria Falls

Victoria Falls is a waterfall on the
Zambezi River, located on the border
between Zambia and Zimbabwe. It is one
of the world's largest waterfalls, with
a width of 1,708m (5,604ft). The region
around it has a high degree of
biodiversity in both plants and
animals.
