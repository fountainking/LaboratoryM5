**HDMI**

HDMI is a brand of proprietary digital
interface used to transmit high-quality
video and audio signals between
devices. It is commonly used to connect
devices such as televisions, computer
monitors, projectors, gaming consoles,
and personal computers. HDMI supports
uncompressed video and either
compressed or uncompressed digital
audio, allowing a single cable to carry
both signals.
