Fiber

Fiber is a natural or artificial
substance that is significantly longer
than it is wide. Fibers are often used
in the manufacture of other materials.
The strongest engineering materials
often incorporate fibers, for example
carbon fiber and
ultra-high-molecular-weight
polyethylene.
