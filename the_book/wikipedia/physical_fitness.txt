Physical fitness

Physical fitness is a state of health
and well-being and, more specifically,
the ability to perform aspects of
sports, occupations, and daily
activities. Physical fitness is
generally achieved through proper
nutrition, moderate-vigorous physical
exercise, and sufficient rest along
with a formal recovery plan.
