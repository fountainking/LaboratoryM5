Multiplication

Multiplication is one of the four
elementary mathematical operations of
arithmetic, with the other ones being
addition, subtraction, and division.
The result of a multiplication
operation is called a product.
Multiplication is often denoted by the
cross symbol, , by the mid-line dot
operator, , by juxtaposition, or, in
programming languages, by an asterisk,
*.
