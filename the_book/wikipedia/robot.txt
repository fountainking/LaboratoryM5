Robot

A robot is a machineespecially one
programmable by a computercapable of
carrying out a complex series of
actions automatically. A robot can be
guided by an external control device,
or the control may be embedded within.
Robots may be constructed to evoke
human form, but most robots are
task-performing machines, designed with
an emphasis on stark functionality,
rather than expressive aesthetics.
