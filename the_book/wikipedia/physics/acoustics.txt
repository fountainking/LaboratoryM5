Acoustics

Acoustics is a branch of physics that
deals with the study of mechanical
waves in gases, liquids, and solids
including topics such as vibration,
sound, ultrasound and infrasound. A
scientist who works in the field of
acoustics is an acoustician while
someone working in the field of
acoustics technology may be called an
acoustical engineer. The application of
acoustics is present in almost all
aspects of modern society with the most
obvious being the audio and noise
control industries.
