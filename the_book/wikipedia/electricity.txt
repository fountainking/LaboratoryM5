Electricity

Electricity is the set of physical
phenomena associated with the presence
and motion of matter possessing an
electric charge. Electricity is related
to magnetism, both being part of the
phenomenon of electromagnetism, as
described by Maxwell's equations.
Common phenomena are related to
electricity, including lightning,
static electricity, electric heating,
electric discharges and many others.
