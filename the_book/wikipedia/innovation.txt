Innovation

Innovation is the practical
implementation of ideas that result in
the introduction of new goods or
services or improvement in offering
goods or services. ISO TC 279 in the
standard ISO 56000:2020 defines
innovation as "a new or changed entity,
realizing or redistributing value".
Others have different definitions; a
common element in the definitions is a
focus on newness, improvement, and
spread of ideas or technologies.
