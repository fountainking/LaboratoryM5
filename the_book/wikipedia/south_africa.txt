South Africa

South Africa, officially the Republic
of South Africa (RSA), is the
southernmost country in Africa. Its
nine provinces are bounded to the south
by 2,798 kilometres of coastline that
stretches along the South Atlantic and
Indian Ocean; to the north by the
neighbouring countries of Namibia,
Botswana, and Zimbabwe; to the east and
northeast by Mozambique and Eswatini;
and it encloses Lesotho.
