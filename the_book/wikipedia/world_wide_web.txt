World Wide Web

The World Wide Web is an information
system that enables content sharing
over the Internet through user-friendly
ways meant to appeal to users beyond IT
specialists and hobbyists. It allows
documents and other web resources to be
accessed over the Internet according to
specific rules of the Hypertext
Transfer Protocol (HTTP).
