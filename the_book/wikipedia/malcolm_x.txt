Malcolm X

MalcolmX was an African American
revolutionary, Muslim minister and
human rights activist who was a
prominent figure during the civil
rights movement until his assassination
by members of the Nation of Islam (NOI)
in 1965. A spokesman for the
organization until 1964, after which he
left the movement, he was a vocal
advocate for Black empowerment and the
promotion of Islam within the African
American community. A controversial
figure accused of preaching violence,
Malcolm X is also a celebrated figure
within African American and Muslim
communities for his pursuit of racial
justice.
