**CRYING**

Category: Actions

Release, grief, emotional cleansing.
Crying in dreams = emotional release.
Healthy processing. Someone else crying
= empathy or projection.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
