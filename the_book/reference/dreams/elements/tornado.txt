**TORNADO**

Category: Elements

Chaos, destructive emotions, upheaval.
Tornadoes destroy to rebuild. Eye of
storm = calm center. Path of
destruction = clearing old.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
