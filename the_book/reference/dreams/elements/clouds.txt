**CLOUDS**

Category: Elements

Thoughts, emotions, obscured vision.
Dark clouds = trouble. White fluffy
clouds = pleasant thoughts. Storm
clouds = brewing emotions.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
