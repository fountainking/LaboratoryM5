**WIND**

Category: Elements

Change, spirit, invisible forces.
Strong wind = major changes. Gentle
breeze = subtle influences. Hurricane =
life upheaval. Calm = peace.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
