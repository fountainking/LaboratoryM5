**FLOOD**

Category: Elements

Overwhelming emotions, cleansing,
destruction. Flood = emotional
overwhelm. Rising water = increasing
pressure. Escape = need to process.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
