**MOON**

Category: Elements

Feminine energy, emotions, cycles. Moon
= unconscious and intuition. Full moon
= completion. New moon = new beginning.
Blood moon = intense emotions.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
