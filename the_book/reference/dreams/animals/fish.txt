**FISH**

Category: Animals

Emotions, unconscious thoughts,
spiritual insights. Fish swim in
emotional waters. Many fish =
abundance. Dead fish = emotional
numbness. Catching = understanding
feelings.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
