**SNAKE**

Category: Animals

Transformation, healing, hidden fears.
Snakes represent change and renewal.
Shedding skin = personal growth. Biting
snake = toxic situation. Dead snake =
overcoming fear.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
