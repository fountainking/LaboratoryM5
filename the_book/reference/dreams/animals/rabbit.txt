**RABBIT**

Category: Animals

Fear, fertility, luck. Rabbits multiply
quickly. Running = anxiety. White
rabbit = following curiosity. Multiple
= creative abundance.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
