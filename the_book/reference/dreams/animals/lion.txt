**LION**

Category: Animals

Courage, leadership, pride. Lions
symbolize personal power and authority.
Roaring = speaking truth. Tamed =
controlled power. Pride of lions =
community strength.

Remember: Dream symbols are deeply
personal. Your own associations and
feelings about symbols matter most. Use
this as a starting point, not absolute
truth.
