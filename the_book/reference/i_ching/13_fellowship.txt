**HEXAGRAM 13: FELLOWSHIP**
Tong Ren

Heaven over Fire

aaa
aaa

KEYWORD: Community, brotherhood, shared
purpose

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Fellowship with others in the open.
Success. It furthers to cross the great
water. Unite with like-minded people.
Shared vision. Community strength. Go
public with your alliance.

**THE IMAGE:**
Heaven together with fire: fellowship.
The superior person organizes the
clans.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Find your tribe. Unite with those who
share your values. Collective power.
Brotherhood/sisterhood. Common cause
creates strength. Be open about your
associations. Together you can cross
great waters.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
