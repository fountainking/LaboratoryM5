**HEXAGRAM 18: WORK ON THE DECAYED**
Gu

Mountain over Wind

aaa
aaa

KEYWORD: Repair, fixing, corruption

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Work on what has been spoiled. Supreme
success. It furthers to cross the great
water. Repair damage. Fix corruption.
Healing decay. Address rot. Difficult
but necessary work. Clean house.

**THE IMAGE:**
Wind at the foot of the mountain:
decay. The superior person stirs up the
people and strengthens the spirit.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Something has rotted. Face it. Clean up
the mess. Repair damage done by neglect
or corruption. Hard work but necessary.
Revive what has decayed. Restore
integrity. Sweep away cobwebs.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
