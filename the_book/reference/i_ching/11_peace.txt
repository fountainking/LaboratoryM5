**HEXAGRAM 11: PEACE**
Tai

Earth over Heaven

a a
aaa

KEYWORD: Harmony, prosperity, spring

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Peace. The small departs, the great
approaches. Good fortune. Success.
Heaven and earth unite. Harmony and
prosperity. Favorable time. Growth and
expansion. Share abundance.

**THE IMAGE:**
Heaven and earth unite: peace. The
sovereign divides and completes the
course of heaven and earth.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Harmonious time. Everything flows
naturally. Heaven and earth in balance.
Prosperity comes. Share your good
fortune. Help others rise. Golden
period but remember nothing lasts
forever.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
