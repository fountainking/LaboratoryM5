**HEXAGRAM 8: HOLDING TOGETHER**
Pi

Water over Earth

a a
a a

KEYWORD: Unity, alliance, partnership

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Holding together brings good fortune.
Inquire of the oracle whether you have
the necessary qualities. Union with
others. Join the right group. Test your
readiness. Together is stronger.

**THE IMAGE:**
Water on the earth: holding together.
Ancient kings bestowed lands and
affiliated with vassals.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Time for alliance and partnership.
Examine if you're ready for commitment.
Right connections bring success. Stand
together. Support each other. Shared
goals unite.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
