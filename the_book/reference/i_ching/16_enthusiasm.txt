**HEXAGRAM 16: ENTHUSIASM**
Yu

Thunder over Earth

a a
a a

KEYWORD: Joy, motivation, music

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Enthusiasm. It furthers to install
helpers and set armies marching. Joy
and motivation. Inspire others. Music
and celebration. Harness enthusiasm for
action. Movement begins.

**THE IMAGE:**
Thunder comes resounding out of the
earth: enthusiasm. The ancient kings
made music and honored the gods.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Burst of enthusiasm. Energy for new
projects. Inspire others with your
passion. Celebrate. Make music. Joyful
action succeeds. Harness this energy
before it fades. Rally the troops.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
