**HEXAGRAM 9: SMALL TAMING**
Xiao Chu

Wind over Heaven

aaa
aaa

KEYWORD: Gentle restraint, minor
obstacles

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Small taming has success. Dense clouds,
no rain from our western region. Minor
restraints. Small obstacles. Gentle
persuasion. Not time for major action.
Accumulate power quietly.

**THE IMAGE:**
Wind drives across heaven: small
taming. The superior person refines the
outward aspect of their nature.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Blocked temporarily but not seriously.
Small frustrations. Refine details.
Prepare but don't launch yet. Gentle
influence works better than force.
Patience with minor delays.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
