**HEXAGRAM 7: THE ARMY**
Shi

Earth over Water

a a
a a

KEYWORD: Discipline, leadership,
collective action

**THE JUDGMENT:**
The army needs perseverance and a
strong leader. Good fortune without
blame. Organized effort required.
Discipline and order. Clear leadership.
United purpose brings success.

**THE IMAGE:**
Water in the middle of the earth: the
army. The superior person increases the
masses by generosity.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Collective effort needed. Organize
resources. Clear hierarchy and roles.
Strong leadership essential. Discipline
yourself and others. United front wins.
Individual action fails.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
