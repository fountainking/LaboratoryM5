**HEXAGRAM 15: MODESTY**
Qian

Earth over Mountain

a a
aaa

KEYWORD: Humility, moderation, balance

**THE JUDGMENT:**
Modesty creates success. The superior
person carries things through. Humility
brings respect. Stay grounded. Balance
extremes. Understate your abilities.
Let results speak.

**THE IMAGE:**
Within the earth, a mountain: modesty.
The superior person reduces excess and
augments what is lacking.

**INTERPRETATION:**
Power of humility. Mountain hides under
earth. Great ability concealed. Don't
boast. Level the playing field. Share
credit. Balance is key. Modest person
succeeds where arrogant fails.

**HOW TO USE THIS HEXAGRAM:**
This hexagram appears when you need its
particular wisdom. Meditate on the
judgment and image. Apply the
interpretation to your current question
or situation.

The I Ching speaks to the moment. What
in your life right now resonates with
this hexagram's teaching?
